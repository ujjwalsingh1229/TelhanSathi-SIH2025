## ğŸ“ Understanding the Output

- **Formula:** Revenue - Total Cost
- **Example:** â‚¹240,000 revenue - â‚¹90,000 cost = â‚¹150,000 profit

- **Formula:** (Profit / Cost) Ã— 100

- **Example:** â‚¹150,000 / â‚¹240,000 = 62.5%
